Conclusions¶

i tried to answer quite a few questions using TMDb data and i find: Which genres are most popular from year to year? Action, Thriller Comedy and Drama are the most popular genres from all movies from 1960 to 2015 and they are presenting 50% of the total movies genres. and foreign, western and documentary genres are the last 3 genres.

Revenue and budget have a weak positive correlation. Revenue and profit have a strong positive correlation. Revenue and popularity have positive correlation, movies with higher revenues is more likely to be more popular

the highest number of movies release in single year is 384 in 2014 and the lowest number is 19 in 1969 , The median number for releasing movies in 1 year is 93 movies.
